# FirstStep Health 🏥

> Turning healthcare skills into legal rural businesses — one clear step at a time.

**Built for Tatweer Hackathon 2026**  
**Challenge 1 – Taking the First Entrepreneurial Step**  
**Al Qua'a, Al Ain, United Arab Emirates**

---

## The Problem

In Al Qua'a, a rural community in Al Ain, people with valuable healthcare skills—such as retired nurses, community first aiders, and health screeners—often struggle to turn those skills into legal businesses.

The problem is not a lack of talent or ambition. The problem is not knowing where to start.

Many aspiring entrepreneurs do not know:

- What licenses or approvals they need
- What documents are required
- How much it costs to start
- Where to find funding opportunities
- What their first step should be

As students and first-time entrepreneurs ourselves, we understand how overwhelming it can be to navigate the process of starting a business without guidance. We built the tool we wished existed.

---

## Evidence of Need

To better understand the problem, we surveyed community members and aspiring entrepreneurs.

Our findings showed that:

- Most respondents did not know what licenses were required to start a healthcare-related business.
- Many were unsure where to find funding information or official guidance.
- Nearly everyone agreed that a simple, step-by-step tool would make starting a business less intimidating.

These findings highlighted a clear information gap and validated the need for an accessible guidance platform.

---

## Our Solution

**FirstStep Health** is an AI-powered guidance platform that helps people turn healthcare skills into legal, community-based businesses.

In under five minutes, users receive a personalised roadmap explaining exactly what they need to do to get started.

The platform provides:

✅ Recommended licenses and approvals

✅ Required documents checklist

✅ Estimated startup costs

✅ Available funding opportunities

✅ A week-by-week action plan

✅ One clear action to take tomorrow

✅ Links to official UAE resources and support organisations

---

## Who It Is For

FirstStep Health is designed for first-time founders in Al Qua'a and other rural UAE communities who have healthcare skills but do not know how to legally establish a business.

---

## How It Works

1. The user describes their healthcare service.
2. The user answers a few simple questions about qualifications, budget, location, and workspace.
3. The AI analyses the information using verified UAE resources.
4. The platform generates a personalised business roadmap.

The user receives:

- License guidance
- Document checklist
- Cost estimate
- Funding recommendations
- Weekly action plan
- Tomorrow's first action

---

## Why FirstStep Health Matters

Our goal is simple:

**No one with valuable healthcare skills should abandon their dream of serving their community simply because they do not know the first step.**

By simplifying business guidance, FirstStep Health empowers rural entrepreneurs, encourages local healthcare initiatives, and promotes community development in Al Qua'a and beyond.
