# Demo Script — FirstStep Health 🏥

## Setup
- Open FirstStep Health on the form page.
- One team member presents while another operates the app.

---

## Story

Meet Fatima. She lives in Al Qua'a and has spent more than 10 years helping her neighbours by checking their blood pressure and blood sugar levels. People trust her, but she has never turned this skill into an official business because she doesn't know where to start.

She doesn't know:
- What license she needs
- What documents are required
- How much it will cost
- Where to get support

This is where FirstStep Health helps.

---

## Live Demo

1. Enter Service:
   "Home blood pressure and diabetes screening"

2. Enter Qualification:
   "Informal health practitioner with 10 years of experience"

3. Select Budget:
   "AED 10,000–30,000"

4. Select Location:
   "Home-based service"

5. Click:
   "Generate My Roadmap"

6. Demonstrate the generated results:
   - Recommended license or approval
   - Required document checklist
   - Estimated startup costs
   - Funding opportunities
   - Week-by-week action plan
   - One action to take tomorrow morning

---

## Closing

In less than five minutes, Fatima goes from feeling overwhelmed to having a clear, personalised roadmap for starting her healthcare service legally.

FirstStep Health transforms uncertainty into action by helping rural healthcare entrepreneurs take their first step toward building sustainable businesses.

**Tagline:** Turning healthcare skills into legal rural businesses — one clear step at a time.
